How we do this:


  • We use ResNet50 pre_trained on ImageNet dataset to classify the given image.

  • To find similar images, we extracted features of all images in COCO-val2014 dataset using pre-trained ResNet50 and we saved them as unlabeled training data. When we are given an image, we extract its feature, and then we find its K-nearest neighbors among unlabeled training data based on Euclidean distance.
